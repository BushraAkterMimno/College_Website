Many people call BHRS College a public college. Many people do not want to say institute. Many people say engineering university. Many people do not want to say science and technology institute.
But the full form of MIST is: "Military Institute of Science & Technology" "Institute" (Institute) "Science & Technology" (Science & Technology) Many people give the example of MIT and say that MIT is also an institute! But all the BSc level colleges and institutes in Bangladesh are affiliated to some public university. The university provides certificates and syllabus. There is no other difference. MIST since BUP affiliated. So BUP provides syllabus and certificates here.
and Engineering & Technology (BUET, CKRUET, DUET) and Science & Technology (SUST, HUSTU, PUST...) Here, science & technology universities also teach engineering subjects. Basically engineering is a part of "science and technology". Again, the general university (DU, RU, JU, CU) is all subjects of science-commerce-arts are taught. Here there is "science and technology" in science; There are also engineering subjects in science and technology!
MIST Bangladesh is administered by the Ministry of Defence (MoD). Bangladesh University of Professionals (BUP) provides certificates at the end of the syllabus and studies So, MIST is a BUP affiliated institute.
